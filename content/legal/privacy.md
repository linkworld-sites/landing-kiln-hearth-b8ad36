# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2026**

Kiln & Hearth ("we," "us," or "our") operates this website. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## What We Collect

**Information you give us:** When you place an order or sign up for our newsletter, we collect your name, email address, shipping address, and payment information. Payment details are processed securely by our payment provider and are not stored by us.

**Information we collect automatically:** We collect basic analytics data — pages visited, time on site, and general location (country level) — to understand how people use our site. We use the Kiln & Hearth funnel tracker for this purpose.

**Cookies:** We use cookies to keep your cart between sessions and to understand site usage. See our Cookie Policy for details.

## How We Use Your Information

- To process and fulfil your orders
- To send order confirmations and shipping updates
- To send our studio newsletter if you subscribed (you can unsubscribe any time)
- To improve our website and understand which pieces people are interested in

## Who We Share It With

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with:

- Our payment processor (to handle transactions)
- Our shipping carrier (to deliver your order)
- Our analytics provider (anonymised data only)

## Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, email us at the address below.
